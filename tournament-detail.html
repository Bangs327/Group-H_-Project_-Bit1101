FASL U-19 Team Prepares for Upcoming Regional Tournament

Our Under-19 team is in intensive training ahead of the major regional youth tournament scheduled for next month. Coaches are focused on fitness, teamwork, and tactical discipline.

Key Dates and Location

The tournament runs from **October 15th to 22nd** in the capital. The squad selection is expected to be finalized by the end of this week.

We encourage all fans and scouts to follow the action closely, as this event is a crucial showcase for our emerging talent.
