FASL Welcomes New Forward, Issa Jalloh, on Long-Term Deal

Football Agency Sierra Leone is delighted to announce the signing of promising 17-year-old striker, Issa Jalloh. Issa joins the agency after an impressive season in the local youth league.

Player Profile

Issa is known for his speed, agility, and clinical finishing ability. He represents a significant investment in the future talent of Sierra Leonean football.

The agency looks forward to developing his career and finding opportunities for him in top international clubs over the next few years.
