FASL Announces Major Partnership with Sierra Juice

Football Agency Sierra Leone (FASL) is thrilled to announce a significant new partnership with Sierra Juice, making them the official beverage sponsor for the upcoming season.

Impact of the Sponsorship

This collaboration will provide essential funding for our youth development programs, allowing us to expand training facilities and offer more scholarships to promising young talent across the region. Sierra Juice is committed to promoting healthy lifestyles and hydration among athletes.

The new sponsorship deal runs for three years and includes branding across all FASL materials and training gear.
